About Us

We, the J³ group from BSIT 1-B, have created a website to raise awareness about gender equality and the organizations driving this movement. Through our website, we aim to inform people about the importance of gender equality and highlight the impactful work done by these organizations. Join us in our mission to promote a more inclusive and equitable society for all.
